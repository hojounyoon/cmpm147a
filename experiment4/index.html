Experiment X - Subject/Topic

Description

I used the infinite extent of my world to represent another mountain with pink and blue flowers and blue mist shrouding over the land:

Technical

I made the main background color green to show we were in a grassland mountain. I made the pink and blue flowers and little dots scattered. I created an eclipse and had some tiles painted blue to depict the blue mist. From the image's perspective, the mist makes the land appear blue.

Reflection

The generated world matches nature's colors from the photo. The shapes of the flowers and mists seem to be well drawn. Of the three infinite worlds I created, this one felt the most realistic, so I chose it as my favorite.
