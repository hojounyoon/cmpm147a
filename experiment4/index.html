Experiment X - Subject/Topic

Description

I used the infinite extent of my world to represent another mountain with pink and blue flowers and blue mist shrouding over the land:

Technical

I made the main background color green to show we were in a grassland mountain. I made the pink and blue flowers and little dots and had them scattered. I created an eclipse and had some tiles painted blue to depict the blue mist. From the image's perspective, the mist can make the land be portrayed as blue.

Reflection

The generated world matches the nature's colors from the photo. The shapes of the flowers and mists seem to be well drawn. Of all the three infinite worlds I created, this one seemed more realistic, so I chose this to be my favorite one
