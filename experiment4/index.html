Experiment X - Subject/Topic

Description

The dungeon world features a randomly generated layout of rooms and hallways.

Technical

Light gray tiles represent the floor while the darker grey tiles represent the wall. The brown square tiles are dark objects stored in the dungeon. Green and red tiles indicate hallways and prison cells and the brown and dark red tiles represent torches that flicker, bringing a sense of life to the scene.

Reflection

Each time the world is generated, the layout changes, giving the impression that the dungeon is alive and always shifting.
